POST MATRIC

Details :

-The Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST (Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe) students is a government scholarship program in India that provides financial assistance to SC/ST students pursuing education at the post-matriculation level, i.e., after completing their secondary education. This scholarship is intended to help students cover the costs of tuition fees, books, and other educational expenses to ensure that they can continue their education.

Objective :

-The primary objective of the Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST (Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe) Students is to provide financial assistance to students belonging to these marginalized communities in India who are pursuing higher education at the post-matriculation level. The scholarship program is designed to address various social and economic barriers that SC/ST students often face and to promote their educational advancement.

Benefits :

-The scholarship typically covers various educational expenses, including tuition fees, maintenance allowance, and other incidental costs.

-The amount of the scholarship can vary, and it is usually based on factors such as the applicant's course of study, location of the institution, and family income.

-The award amount can vary according to the class in which the applicant is studying.

Eligibility :

-To be eligible, an applicant must:

-Belong to the Scheduled Caste category.

-Be a permanent resident of Gujarat state.

-Be studying in Graduation to Postgraduation from a recognized college, university, or institute.

-Have an annual family income of not more than INR 2.5 lakhs from all sources.

-Not be a repeater in the same class more than once.

Documents :

1.Passport size photograph of the applicant.

2.Self-attested caste certificate issued by the competent authorities
Self-attested copy of the Aadhaar Card.

3.The first page of bank passbook/cancelled cheque.

4.Self-attested certificate of previous academic mark sheet.

5.Disability certificate (applicable for persons with disability)
Fee receipt of current academic course year.

6.Break affidavit (If break gap is more than one year).

7.Income certificate issued by a competent authority (Form no.16 required for Govt. Employee).

8.Thesis typing/printing bill (applicable for MPhil, PhD, D.Litt, D.Pharm, D.Sc students).

9.School leaving certificate.

10.Domicile certificate (for Gujarati origin SC student studying in outstate).

11.Hostel Certificate (applicable for hosteler students).

12.LWP (Leave without pay) certificate (for employed students).

Application process :

-Eligible candidates can apply for the scholarship through the following steps:

Step 1: Click on the 'Apply Now' button below.

Step 2: Fill in the details and register.

Step 3: After successful registration, log in with username and password.

Step 4: Fill in the scholarship application form and upload the required documents.

Step 5: Finally submit the application form.
