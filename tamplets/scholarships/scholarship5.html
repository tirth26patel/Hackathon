PER MATRIC

Details :

-The Government of Gujarat provides Pre-Matric Scholarships for Scheduled Caste (SC) and Scheduled Tribe (ST) students in Gujarat. These scholarships are designed to support students from these communities in their pre-matric education, which generally includes classes from 1 to 10.

Objective :

-The primary objective to offering scholarships at the pre-matric level, the government aims to encourage students to complete their basic education, which is a critical foundation for their future development.

-This scholarship program helps reduce dropout rates by covering various educational expenses, such as tuition fees, exam fees, and other related costs.

-By providing financial aid, the government aims to empower SC and ST students to access quality education and improve their socioeconomic status.

-The Pre-Matric Scholarship program promotes inclusive growth by ensuring that students from SC and ST communities have equal opportunities for education.

Eligibility :

-The applicant must belong to either the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) community.

-The student should be studying in a government or recognized private school in Gujarat.

-The annual family income of the student's parents or guardians should not exceed the prescribed limit. This limit may vary from year to year, so it's essential to check the current income limit specified by the Gujarat government.Nowadays,it is 2,50,000 INR.

Application Process :

Step 1:Visit the official website of the Government of Gujarat's Education Department or the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).

Step 2:Register or log in to the scholarship portal.

Step 3:Fill out the application form with accurate information.

Step 4:Upload the necessary documents, including caste certificate, income certificate, and academic records.

Step 5:Submit the application within the specified deadline.

Documents Required :

-Caste Certificate

-Income Certificate

-Domicile Certificate

-Aadhaar Card

-Bank Account Details

-Educational Certificate

-Passport-sized Photograph

-Bonafide Certificate
