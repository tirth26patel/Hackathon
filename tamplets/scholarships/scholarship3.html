NATIONAL OVERSEAS SCHOLARSHIP

Details :

-The National Overseas Scholarship for SC/ST (Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe) Students is a scholarship program in India designed to provide financial assistance to SC/ST students who wish to pursue higher education at the postgraduate or doctoral level in foreign universities. This scholarship is administered by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India. The key objectives of this scholarship are to enable SC/ST students to access world-class education abroad and enhance their knowledge and skills for their academic and career growth.

Objectives :

-The scholarship aims to provide opportunities for SC/ST students to pursue postgraduate or doctoral studies in reputable foreign universities, thereby gaining access to high-quality education that may not be available in India.

-By studying abroad, students can acquire advanced knowledge and skills in their choosen fields, which can contribute to their personal and professional growth.

-The program helps empower SC/ST communities by providing opportunities for higher education abroad, which can lead to better career prospects and increased socio-economic mobility.

Features :

-The scholarship typically covers a range of expenses, including tuition fees, living expenses, and one-time settlement allowances for selected candidates.

-Applicants must belong to the SC/ST category and have a consistent academic record.

-The selection of candidates is usually done through a rigorous process that includes an evaluation of their academic and research qualifications, recommendation letters, and an interview.

-The scholarship generally supports a wide range of academic disciplines and fields of study, enabling students to pursue their desired courses abroad.

Eligibility :

-Minority community candidates applying for the National Overseas Scholarship must fulfill the requirements given below:

-For Masters : minimum 55% or it's equivalent at the undergraduate level.

-For Doctorate : minimum 55% or it's equivalent at the master's level.

-Applicants must be below 35 years.

-Total family income should not exceed 6,00,000 INR.

-Only two children from the family can avail the National Overseas Scholarship.

Expenses covered under scholarship:

-Maintenance allowance.

-Contingency allowance.

-Poll tax.

-Incidental Journey and Equipment allowance.

-Visa fees.

-Tuition fees.

-Medical insurance premium.

-Air passage.

-Local travel.

Documents Required :

-10th board certificate

-Caste certificate

-Photo

-Scanned signature

-Current Address proof/Permanent Address proof

-Qualifying Degree/provisional certificate

-Marksheet of qualifying examination

-Income documents of all family members

-Employer's NOC certificate if applicant is employed.

-Degree of applicant

-ITR acceptance document

-Aadhar card
